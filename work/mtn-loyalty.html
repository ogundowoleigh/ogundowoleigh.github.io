MTN Loyalty

MTN is the largest mutlinational mobile telecommunications company in West Africa. They intended to kick of a customer loyalty program to recognize and reward customers who purchase or engage with a brand on a recurring basis.

This project was created as part of a pitch. I was the Senior UI/UX Designer at the time, working with a Junior designer on the UI for the mobile app designed to help the customers manage and spend their points. I also worked with the Business Development team on strategy.


The mobile app

Users were classified into 3 main categories(customers, administrators, merchants). For the pitch, the interface focused primarily on the mobile app for the customers. Here were a few feature narratives that we suggested would work:

  • Allow individual subscribers monitor their reward points in real-time
  • Show lists of merchant and services etc.
  • Claim rewards (virtual and physical reward)
  • Get notifications about system events(new rewards, level upgrade, messages)
  • Top-up their accounts
  • Get notifications and directions of merchants in proximity
  • View claimed deals history
